Doner Kebab

Description

Doner Kebab is a traditional Turkish dish made with seasoned meat cooked on a vertical rotisserie. It's typically served in a wrap or pita bread with vegetables and sauces. This recipe brings the authentic flavors of Turkey to your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Ground lamb or beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Yogurt
  • Olive oil
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Pita bread or flatbread
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Tzatziki sauce or garlic sauce

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground lamb or beef, finely chopped onion, minced garlic, yogurt, olive oil, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Cover the bowl and let the meat marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.
  3. Preheat your grill or oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. Shape the marinated meat into a large loaf and place it on a rotisserie skewer, or on a baking sheet for the oven.
