Kebab

Description

Kebap is a popular Middle Eastern dish made with skewered and grilled meat, often served with vegetables and a variety of sides. This recipe offers a delicious and authentic taste of kebab that is sure to satisfy.

Ingredients

  • Ground lamb or beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Sumac
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Skewers

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground lamb or beef, finely chopped onion, minced garlic, chopped parsley, sumac, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Form the meat mixture into long, thin kebab shapes around the skewers. Brush with olive oil.
  4. Grill the kebabs for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  5. Serve hot with your choice of sides such as rice, salad, or flatbread.
